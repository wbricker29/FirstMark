# AI Builder Role - FirstMark Capital

## Overview
FirstMark Capital is seeking an AI Builder to lead the firm's AI transformation from the ground up. This is a greenfield opportunity to build AI capabilities, workflows, and agent-based systems across investing, platform, and back office operations.

## Key Responsibilities

**Strategy & Infrastructure**
- Set the vision and roadmap for AI implementation across the firm
- Design and build data architecture that consolidates and structures information for AI applications
- Evaluate and implement appropriate tools and technologies

**AI Solutions Development**
Build AI workflows and automation for three core areas:

*Investing*
- Automate first drafts of deal memos and investment memos
- Streamline valuation analysis

*Platform*
- Optimize event guest lists and invitations
- Cultivate and manage networks
- Repurpose content from events into blog posts and social media
- Support talent management initiatives

*Back Office & Portfolio Management*
- Enhance IR and finance operations
- Analyze board decks and benchmark portfolio companies
- Track key metrics

**Team & Execution**
- Potentially manage flexible offshore development team
- Deliver tangible value quickly with pragmatic solutions

## Ideal Candidate Profile
- **Business engineer** mindset: understands both technical implementation and venture capital business context
- Proven experience building agent-based workflows and AI systems
- Ability to work independently on greenfield projects
- Technology-agnostic approach focused on solving problems rather than specific tools
- Comfortable creating structure and scaffolding where none exists

## Logistics
- **Location:** Flatiron office, New York
- **Work arrangement:** 5 days in-office
- **Timeline:** Targeting hire by end of year
